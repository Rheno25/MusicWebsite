Profile Video

Malang, a city located in East Java, Indonesia, has a rich history in music that spans back several decades. The music scene in Malang is diverse, with influences from traditional Javanese music, Western music, and other Indonesian musical genres.

Talent

Bayu Skak

Bayu Eko Moektito or often called “Bayu Skak”, is an actor, director, scriptwriter, YouTuber, comedian, singer, and composer born in Indonesia, East Java, Malang City. He started his career by starting his YouTube channel in 2009 and is currently at 3 million subscribers. Starting his career by making his image as a comedian, many people wouldn’t expect this person to start a music career. His talent as a musician and singer has been proven by performing in the movie “Yowis Ben” and “Lara Ati” which was a big success for him and the debut of his music career.

Krisdayanti

Krisdayanti (Kris Dayanti) is a singer, actress, and currently, an Indonesian politician, is a talent that was born on March 24th, 1975 in Batu, Malang. She started her career in the early 12 years of her life releasing her first album “Biasa Saja”, she doesn’t have quite a good head start. But all of that changed when she joined the recording label “Warner Music Indonesia” and released her first-ever professional album “Terserah” which sold quite well to the public. The peak of her career was proved by the success of her first-ever solo concert in 2001 which gave her the title “Indonesian Pop Diva” embedded in her name.

Andhika Pratama

Andhika Pratama Subagyo is an actor, presenter, comedian, and singer that was born in Malang, East Java. He started his career by being a vocalist in a band that performed from one cafe to another. While he started his career as a musician, the peak of his career was when he signed a contract with his talent agency and became a big actor and presenter. His name became famous due to his collaboration with Melly Goeslaw for a duet in a theme song for the movie “The Butterfly” as well as being an actor in that movie.

Sal Priadi

Salmantyo Ashrizky Priadi, or known as Sal Priadi is a singer, composer, and actor from Malang, East Java, Indonesia. Sal started his music career in 2015 by uploading his covers and music into SoundCloud, helped by Mahatamtama and Derry from the music group “Coldiac”. He started his professional debut by releasing his first-ever single called “Kultusan” through GZZ Records in 2017. His name became well known when he released his first album “Berhati” on February 20th, 2020 with the help of a famous producer called “Ari Renaldi”. Not just that but he was also nominated for “Best Male Pop Solo Artist” at the “Indonesian Music Awards 2018”.

Keisya Levronka

If we’re talking about musicians from Malang, it’ll be different if we didn’t mention the current hit musician, Keisya Levronka. Keisya started her involvement in the entertainment world by participating in a talent show called “Indonesian Idol Season 10”. After her performance in Indonesian Idol, she joined the label “Universal Music Indonesia” and released her first-ever single called “Jadi Kekasihku Saja”. Her name legitimately boomed after her fourth single “Tak Ingin Usai” which was released in 2022 succeeded in being on the top of the billboard for 11 weeks and being the most played music on Indonesian digital music platforms.

History of Music in Malang

  • 1960s Era

    The early period of music development in Malang City, which can also be called the golden era, began in the mid-1960s. At that time there were several bands that were the prima donna and subscriptions of event owners. The influence of western music that entered Indonesia in the 1960s was quickly absorbed by musicians from Malang. Stages for performances in the early 1960s era also appeared. They are the Flora Building or Flora Cinema, Tjendrawasih Building or what is now better known as the Gajayana Art Building. Etc.

  • 1970s Era

    New bands carrying the rock genre which gradually continues to develop with various variants. The influence of rock music from abroad at that time also influenced the naming of the bands in the 1970s which tended to be westernized. During the time of Mayor Soegiyono or who is familiarly called Ebes Soegiyono around 1974-1975 a sports building (GOR) was built called Pulosari GOR. Besides that, there is also Indrokilo Park or Tanaka Park (during the Japanese colonial era) which is behind the Brawijaya Museum.

  • 1980s Era

    Entering the mid-1980s era, the development of music in Malang City was still focused on rock music but showed progress to play the Heavy Metal genre. The arena for performances in the 1980s was still the same as in previous eras, namely at the Pulosari Sports Hall. In the era of the 1980s Pulosari Sports Hall still existed, this was shown when God Bless held a tour in 1986 to Malang City.

  • 1990s Era

    In the mid-1990s, the development of music in Malang City continued to show its fangs. Rocker and more fierce shows the musical tastes of Malang City youths in this era. For example, Heavy Metal, Speed Metal, and Trash Metal were worshiped by children in the past. In the 1990s, the music community also showed its existence. For example, Gemma (Generation of Young Musicians from Malang City) and HM3 (Association of Young Musicians from Malang City) continued to accommodate the emergence of bands in the 1990s.

  • 2000s Era

    The mid-2000s era was a transitional period for music movements from the 1990s to the 2000s. The underground music movement began to show its capacity in terms of playing music and organizing an event. Hangout places during the transition from the 1990s to the 2000s were also found in several places. Such as in the parking lot behind Sarinah, Tugu Chairil Anwar, Mitra 2, Antz Studio, GOR Pulosari, and many more. Then the Sasana Asih YPAC Building, the DKM (Malang Arts Council) Building, the KNPI Building, Jagrak Tengah Cafe, Scout Building, Swing Cafe, and many more.
